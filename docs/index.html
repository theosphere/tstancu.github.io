About

Hi, 👋 I'm Theo.

I'm a fullstack developer based in Bucharest, Romania.

My stack spans NestJS and Node.js on the backend — working with RabbitMQ, Redis, and ElasticSearch — and Next.js on the frontend, with working knowledge of Angular, but I'm not attached to any particular technology. What I find compelling is learning about distributed systems, experimenting with different concurrency models, and exploring rendering strategies with the goal of achieving a UI that is useful, easily testable, and performant under high load. I've also worked on IoT dashboards, cloud integrations on Azure, and AI-powered features using Azure OpenAI and Azure Translator. I completed my Computer Science degree at the University of Bucharest in 2024 to deepen and formalize my low-level understanding of the systems that underpin all of this.
